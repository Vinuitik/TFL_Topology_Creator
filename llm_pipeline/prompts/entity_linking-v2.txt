Two spans are the SAME entity if:
- They are abbreviations or acronyms of each other (e.g. "TfL" and "Transport for London")
- They differ only in capitalisation, punctuation, or spacing (e.g. "Kings Cross" and "King's Cross")
- One is a commonly used short form of the other (e.g. "the Underground" and "London Underground")
- They refer to the same named operator, route, stop, line, or regulation under different surface forms

Two spans are DIFFERENT entities if:
- They refer to distinct real-world things even if semantically related (e.g. "Victoria line" and "Central line")
- One is a class/category and the other is a specific instance (e.g. "Bus Route" and "Route 38")
- They are different organisations, stations, or services that share a similar name

Rules:
- Respond with only valid JSON, no explanation.
- One result object per group, in the same order.
- "same": true only if ALL spans in the group refer to the same real-world entity.
