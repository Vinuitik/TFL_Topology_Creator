
Which stations are served by a given underground line, and in which transport zones are they located?

SELECT DISTINCT ?station ?zone
WHERE {
  ?station a ex:TrainStation .
  ?station ex:servedByLine ?line .
  ?station ex:hasZone ?zone .

  FILTER(?line = ex:CentralLine)
}
ORDER BY ?station ?zone

Which stations are directly connected to a given station, and on which lines do these
connections occur?

SELECT DISTINCT ?connectedStation ?route
WHERE {
  {
    ?segment a ex:RouteSegment .
    ?segment ex:fromStop ?station .
    ?segment ex:toStop ?connectedStation .
  }
  UNION
  {
    ?segment a ex:RouteSegment .
    ?segment ex:toStop ?station .
    ?segment ex:fromStop ?connectedStation .
  }

  ?segment ex:partOfRoute ?route .

  FILTER(?station = ex:KingCross)  # replace with station IRI
}
ORDER BY ?connectedStation

Which stations are located within a specific transport zone, and what services operate in that
zone?

SELECT DISTINCT ?station ?service
WHERE {
  ?station a ex:TrainStation .
  ?station ex:hasZone ?zone .
  ?station ex:servedByLine ?service .

  FILTER(?zone = ex:Zone1)  # replace with your zone IRI
}
ORDER BY ?station ?service
