For the iCity ontology, instead of downloading a .ttl or .owl file, you do the following:
1. Open Protege
2. Click on File
3. Click Open from URL
4. Paste the IRI of the iCity ontology

Now the iCity ontology is a bit more confusing, instead of having just one ontology that you open, there are multiple IRIs.
The basic ontology: http://ontology.eil.utoronto.ca/icity/OTN/
The Urban System ontology: http://ontology.eil.utoronto.ca/icity/UrbanSystem/
The Activity Ontolgy: http://ontology.eil.utoronto.ca/icity/UrbanSystem/

Usually you would load the other ontologies together
